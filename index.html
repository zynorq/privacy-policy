Privacy Policy | BLOCK PUZZLE

Introduction

BLOCK PUZZLE respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. The game is designed to provide a safe and enjoyable experience for all users.

Information Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data such as name, email, or device information. All gameplay data remains stored locally on your device only.

Data Processing

All game processes, including scores and progress, are handled locally without external servers.

Permissions

The game does not request sensitive permissions such as camera, location, or microphone.

Security

Your data remains fully secure on your device since no personal information is collected or transmitted.

Internet Usage

The core gameplay of BLOCK PUZZLE works offline and does not require an internet connection. However, an internet connection may be used in future updates for optional features such as advertisements, analytics, or additional services that improve the game experience. These features are not required to play the game.

Third-Party Services

Currently, the game does not use third-party services. In future updates, we may integrate trusted third-party services such as advertising networks or analytics tools to support development and improve user experience. Any such services will comply with privacy regulations.

Ads Policy

BLOCK PUZZLE currently contains no advertisements. In future updates, we may introduce non-intrusive ads to support ongoing development while keeping the game free for users.

Children's Privacy

The game is suitable for all ages and does not intentionally collect any personal data from children or adults.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
